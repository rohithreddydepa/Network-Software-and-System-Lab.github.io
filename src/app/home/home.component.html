The Network, Software and System(NSS) Laboratory
College of Technology

University of Houston

The Network, Software and System (NSS) Laboratory is situated in Room 228D, Technology Building 2, at the University of Houston. As a hub of innovation and learning, the NSS Laboratory not only spearheads cutting-edge research but also serves as a premier educational center dedicated to cultivating the next generation of experts in networking, software, and systems science. The facility boasts an array of high-performance servers, advanced network devices, and robust workstations. These resources are meticulously tailored to support complex model development, in-depth analysis of trust management algorithms, and comprehensive simulations, as well as rigorous evaluations and testing protocols.
